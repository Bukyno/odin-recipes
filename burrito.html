Burrito

Description

A simple and very quick recipe for beef burritos with beans, offering excellent nutritional value and amazing taste.

Ingredients

  • 400g ground beef
  • Tortilla wraps
  • Can of red beans
  • 2 pieces of onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • White yoghurt

Steps

  1. Heat up the pan and add the meat.
  2. Chop the onion and add it to the meat in the pan.
  3. After about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, add the beans.
  4. Prepare the tortilla, place the meat mixture on the tortilla, add yogurt, and wrap.
