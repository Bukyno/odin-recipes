Curry

Description

This chicken curry with chickpeas and rice is a rich, flavorful dish that’s easy to prepare and perfect for a cozy lunch or dinner. The creamy sauce made with curry spices, paprika, and a hint of chili wraps tender chicken and hearty chickpeas in a comforting meal.

It’s a satisfying recipe that combines protein, creaminess, and mild heat. Served with fragrant rice, it’s a simple dish that feels indulgent and wholesome at the same time.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts (or thighs), cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 can of chickpeas (approx. 400g), rinsed and drained
  • 200 ml heavy cream (or cooking cream)
  • 1–2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • A pinch of chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooking oil (vegetable or sunflower oil)
  • Basmati or jasmine rice (for serving)

Steps

  1. Cook the rice according to the instructions on the package. Optionally, rinse it beforehand to prevent clumping.
  2. Heat a bit of oil in a large pan or pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add the chicken pieces to the pan, season with a bit of salt, and cook until they start to brown on all sides.
  4. Sprinkle in the curry powder, paprika, and chili powder. Stir well and let the spices cook for about 30 seconds to release their flavor.
  5. Add the rinsed chickpeas and Worcestershire sauce. Stir everything together.
  6. Pour in the cream and mix well. Let the curry simmer for about 10–15 minutes, until the sauce thickens slightly and the chicken is fully cooked.
  7. Taste and adjust the seasoning with more salt or chili if needed.
  8. Serve hot over a bed of rice. Optionally, garnish with fresh herbs like cilantro or parsley.
